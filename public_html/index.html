Made by George Cadwallader

Botanist - an expert in or student of the scientific study of plants.

Start your plant learning journey by clicking the 'Start' button below. Once started click and drag your mouse to move up the plant, click on the information icons to learn more about the part of the plant you are currently at.

Use the speaker icons to have an audio description of the information section you are viewing. The button to toggle the ambient music is at the bottom right of the screen.
